Spicy Salmon Bowl

Ingredients:

  • Salmon
  • Rice
  • Avocado(or any other vegatable)
  • Sauce

Preparation:

  1. Dice the salmon
  2. Prepare the sauce
  3. Marinate the salmon and season with salt&pepper, garlic powder, cayanne pepper and paprika (optional: add more chilli)
  4. Put the marinated salmon cubes into air frier or an oven (you can also use a pan)
  5. Air fry at 200° or oven bake at 220° for 10-12 minutes
  6. Prepare your vegatables
