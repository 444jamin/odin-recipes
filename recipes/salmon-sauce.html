Sweeet & Spicy Salmon Sauce

Ingredients:

  • Brown sugar
  • Ketchup
  • Soy sauce
  • Worchester sauce
  • Sesame seed oil
  • Ice cubes

Preparation:

  1. Melt a bit of brown sugar on a low heat pan
  2. Add ketchup as soon as it starts melting
  3. Add any spicy sauce you have
  4. Add soy & worchester sauce
  5. Add sesame seed oil
  6. (optional) Add a couple ice cubes for thickness
